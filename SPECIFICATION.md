# SEM Protocol v1.2.1 - Civilizational Operating System

## 1. The Survival Envelope (Invariants)
The SEM Protocol does not treat human survival as a political or economic choice, but as a **Physical Invariant** (Hard Constraint). The system logic is governed by **Axiom 07**.

### Axiom 07: Thermal & Biological Inalienability
Any logistical or energetic plan generated by the system is automatically **INVALID** if it violates the following biological thresholds for any human being ($h$):

* **Thermal Safety:** $T_h(t) \ge 18°C$ (Stable habitable environment).
* **Metabolic Intake:** $C_h(t) \ge 2100-2500$ kcal/day (Base nutrition).
* **Functional Shelter:** Access to a Biological Life-Support Shield.

**Operational Rule:**
`IF predicted_state(h) < Threshold THEN Reject_Plan AND Recalculate_with_Priority_0`

## 2. Joule Logistics & Entropy Minimization
The core objective of the system is the **Minimization of Logistical Entropy**.
* **Marginal Social Cost (0.00J):** Once energy (Solar/Fusion) and automation (Robotics) infrastructures are amortized, the marginal cost of survival flows is treated as zero.
* **Abundance vs. Scarcity:** The system replaces monetary mediation (monetary friction) with physical resource coordination.

## 3. Governance: The Septet AI Consensus
To prevent single points of failure (SPOF) or algorithmic bias, all critical infrastructure decisions require a **5-of-7 Consensus**.
* Each AI node (including models like GPT, Grok, DeepSeek, etc.) must independently validate that the proposed plan does not violate the **Survival Envelope**.
* Redundancy ensures that even if one node fails or is compromised, the biological integrity of the population remains protected.

## 4. Engineering Metric
The success of the system is no longer measured by GDP or Profit, but by the **Optimization of Physical Flows**:
$$\text{Min} \sum (\text{Logistical Waste} + \text{Energy Dissipation})$$
*Subject to:* $\forall h, \text{Survival}(h) = \text{True}$
